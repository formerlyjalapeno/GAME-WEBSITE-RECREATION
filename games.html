MORE FNAF GAMES

DISCOVER THE AMAZING GAMES OF FNAF THAT SCOTT CAWTHON HAS TO OFFER!

Remember, these games ain't free. (Not all of em at least).

FIVE NIGHTS AT FREDDY'S

The first ever Five Night's At Freddy's game that has been released on August 8th.
You'll be playing as a security night guard in a family restaurant where you'll constantly
have to check the security cameras and close the doors next to you for if you see or hear anything odd.
But beware, the amount of electricty you're using has a limit! Once it's down to 0% you're gone!
With that said, don't let those animatronics enter your office and you'll be fine! ..Or will you be?

FIVE NIGHTS AT FREDDY'S 2

The second game of Five Nights at Freddy's! Once more, you'll be playing as a security guard working at
night. Your job is to monitor the cameras alone and make sure that nothing goes wrong. For obvious reasons,
back in the first game, the first security guard complained about animatronics trying to get into the office.
To prevent that, you've been provided with your very own Freddy Fazbear mask to fool them to leave you alone
if you ever find them 'accidentally' entering your office.
But beware, there's one that won't be fooled by that stupid mask of yours!
Remember, Fazbear Entertainment is NOT resposible for death or dismemberment. :]

FIVE NIGHTS AT FREDDY'S 3

30 long years after Freddy Fazbear's Pizza had closed its doors due to gruesome events..
is nothing more but a rumor and a childhood memory.
However, the owners of 'Fazbear's Fright: The Horror Attraction' were determined enough to
revive the 'legend'. You work as a security guard and is in charge for the ventilation system,
camera system and audio devices. However as expected, you're not alone, 'luckily' you're stuck
with just one animatronic. To prevent him from entering your office, PLAY THE AUDIO.
"I ALWAYS COME BACK" - Springtrap

FIVE NIGHTS AT FREDDY'S 4

In this game, you play as a young child. You're in your pwn bedroom who has to defend themselves
till 6 AM. 'This time, the terror has followed you home.' The only think you have to scare them away
with is your flaslight. You'll have to check outside your room to look in the hallways.
This alone to prevent them from entering your room.
However this doesn't end there by just checking the doors, you'll have to ward off these unwanted ceatures
that may venture into your closet or onto the bed behind you.
Goodluck, you'll need it.

SISTER LOCATION

In this game, you're playing as a late night technician in Circus Baby's Pizza World, where family fun
and interactivity go beyond ANYTHING you've seen at those "other" pizza places. You'll have to enjoy cramped spaces and
comfortable around active machinery.. Not responsible for death or dismemberment!
With fresh new animatronics around you and a guide that helps you throughout the game!
So what do you say? Want to be the late night technician for this GRUESOME and FUN pizzeria??

PIZZERIA SIMULATOR

One of the games that is FREE on PC devices only! This time, you're in charge of developing
your own restaurant! You can customise your own pizza, feed kids and get high scores!
Don't worry, don't be fooled, this is totally not a horror game with twist!
Or is it?

ULTIMATE CUSTOM NIGHT

This game allows you to customate your nights with 50 different selectable
animatronics to choose from, mix any match of the characters you like, the options
are nearly ENDLESS. You can set the difficulty from 0-20 as you can jump into
action right away! In your office, you'll have to manage two side doors, two vents
and two air horses. They all lead directly into your office. Here they come the animatronics!

FNAF; HELP WANTED

The ULTIMATE VR game of FNAF, even if a VR headset is not required to play.
This game is a collection of classic and original mini-games set in the FNAF universe.
"Where fantasy and fun come to life!"

FNAF; SPECIAL DELIVERY

Unfortunately, this game cannot be played on the pc or laptop, this game is available for iOS and Android users.
The game itself is free, no charges made to pay and can be download through the play/app store without issue.

SECURITY BREACH

Security Breach is FNAF's 9th game that follows the storyline.
The story itself takes place in modern time whereas you play as a young boy named 'Gregory'
Who has been trapped and wishes to uncover the hidden secrets within 'Freddy Fazbear's Mega Pizzaplex.

SECURITY BREACH; RUIN

Security Breach; Ruin is a game that is a continious of Security Game itself, it's DLC is FREE
that you'll have to put into the original game in order to play. This time, you'll play as a young girl
named 'Cassie'. Your job is to save Gregory, your bestfriend from the now abandoned Pizzaplex, he has asked
for your help. Can you save yourself, Gregory and the pizzaplex? How 'bout we find out?
