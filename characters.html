CHARACTERS

The animatronics are not your friends, at Five Nights at Freddy's your life depends.

"I've been living in shadows. There is only one thing left for me to do now. I'm going to come find you. I'm going to come find you..."


Micheal Afton


Michael Afton is a central character in the "Five Nights at Freddy's" series. He is the son of
William Afton, the Purple Guy, and plays a crucial role in the storyline, particularly in "Sister
Location." His journey involves trying to rescue his sister and uncovering the mysteries of his
haunted family.

"I always come back! You may not recognize me at first, but I assure you, it's still me."

William Afton


William Afton is a pivotal character in the "Five Nights at Freddy's" series, often referred to as
the Purple Guy due to his distinctive attire. He is the main antagonist, responsible for creating
the animatronics and being involved in sinister activities related to the haunted pizzerias. Afton's
actions drive much of the series' dark and mysterious narrative, making him a key figure in the lore
of "Five Nights at Freddy's."
